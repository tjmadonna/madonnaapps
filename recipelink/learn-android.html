How to share recipes from my web browser?

Chrome

From the recipe web page, press the three dots in the upper right corner.

From the list of options, press "Share...".

From the share menu, press "Add Recipe".

Firefox

From the recipe web page, press the three dots in the upper right corner.

From the list of options, press share button on the top left of the list.

From the share menu, press "Add Recipe".

Other?

Recipe Link supports all major Android web browsers. If you have any questions please email developer@madonnaapps.com for support.
